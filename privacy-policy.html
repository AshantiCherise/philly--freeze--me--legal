Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Philly Freeze Me Supply Portal ("the App") is operated by Philly Freeze Me ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and role within the organization. Employees, supervisors, and warehouse staff authenticate with a name and PIN rather than email.
  • Profile Data: Optional profile photo or avatar emoji you choose to set.
  • Order & Inventory Data: Orders placed, inventory quantities, invoices, and related business records.
  • Chat Messages: Text messages and photos sent through the in-app chat feature.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens for delivering order updates and alerts.
  • Crash Data: Anonymous crash logs and diagnostics to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the supply portal — processing orders, managing inventory, and generating invoices.
  • To enable in-app communication between team members.
  • To send push notifications about order status changes and important updates.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app performance.
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.

3. Biometric Authentication

The App supports Face ID and Touch ID for secure sign-in. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We never receive, store, or have access to your biometric data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication & Cloud Firestore (Google) — Account management and data storage. Firebase Privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — Anonymous crash reporting. No personally identifiable information is included in crash logs.
  • GIPHY — GIF search within chat. Search queries are sent to GIPHY's API. GIPHY Privacy
  • Stripe — Payment processing for invoices. Payment details are handled directly by Stripe and are never stored in our systems. Stripe Privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is shared only:

  • With the third-party services listed above, solely to operate the App's features.
  • Within the organization — for example, chat messages are visible to the conversation participants, and order data is visible to roles involved in fulfillment.
  • If required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you or an administrator deletes your account, your user record, chat history, and associated data are permanently removed from our systems.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App by navigating to your profile and selecting "Delete Account." This permanently removes:

  • Your user profile and credentials
  • All chat conversations and messages you participated in
  • Your authentication record

Order and invoice records may be retained for business record-keeping purposes.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Credentials stored in the iOS Keychain (encrypted, hardware-backed storage)
  • PIN rate limiting and automatic session timeouts
  • Firestore security rules that restrict data access by user role
  • App Transport Security enforced for all network connections

9. Children's Privacy

The App is a business tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
